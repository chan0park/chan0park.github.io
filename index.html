Research Interests

My research aims to enhance the effectiveness, fairness, and adaptability of language technologies for diverse populations by centering social context in NLP systems. More specifically,

  • NLP: Improving and adapting NLP models with social contexts such as culture and community
  • AI Ethics: Evaluating NLP models to monitor their fairness and safety across diverse languages and cultures
  • Computational Social Science: Using NLP models to understand social phenomena such as misinformation and social movement
